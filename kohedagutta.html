Koheda gutta

The Koheda gutta Hanuman Temple on the outskirts of Hyderabad is a popular religious site for Hindus in the region. Dedicated to Lord Hanuman, one of the most revered deities in Hindu mythology. This temple is believed to have been built over 250 years ago. Thousands of devotees visit the temple each year to seek blessings, offer prayers, and participate in various ceremonies and celebrations. It is considered as one of the best hidden picnic shot of Hyderabad. If you are looking for a nice 1 hour roadtrip near ORR then Kheda gutta Hanuman Temple is one of the destination to visit.

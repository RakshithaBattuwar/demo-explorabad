secretariat

Telangana Secretariat, officially known as Dr. B. R. Ambedkar Telangana Secretariat situated at Hyderabad, is the administrative office of the employees of the Government of Telangana in India. The Telangana government on 15 September 2022 has decided to name the new Secretariat complex as Dr. B. R. Ambedkar Telangana Secretariat.[1].Designed by Chennai-based Architects Ponni Concessao and Oscar Concessao,the complex has been built by Shapoorji Pallonji and Company Pvt Limited with all Green Building Council norms.
